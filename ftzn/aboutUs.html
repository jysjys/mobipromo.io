團隊背景

MobiPromo公司創立於2010年，歷經3年研究開發，成為市場上領先的無線網絡營銷系統，主要業務遍布澳大利亞、美國、東南亞、南非、中國大陸、香港。從2017年開始，MobiPromo公司與國內外移動互聯網精英結合組成CAN團隊，致力於將移動互聯網與區塊鏈技術相結合，打造遍布於全球的內容分發與廣告分發網絡。

特邀專家

Henry Yu

Practising blockchain lawyer and early Bitcoin adopter. With over 15 years of legal experience in various international law firms and leading investment bank. Founded his own law firm, L&Y Law Office (www.lylawoffice.com), to provide legal services to the blockchain and cryptocurrency communities. Appointed as the Hon. Legal Advisor to the Hong Kong Federation of Invention and Innovation and to the Institute of Financial Technologists of Asia. Member of the Technology Committee of the Hong Kong Law Society. Graduated in King's College, University of London (LLB, AKC) and then the University of Hong Kong (PCLL). Has been interviewed by CNBC and other blockchain media.
